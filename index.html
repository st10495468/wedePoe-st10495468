Sweet Crumbs Bakery

We are a family-run artisan bakery in Durban we were established in 2018 by our pastry chef Leila Jacobs. With an emphasis on organic, locally sourced ingredients, we specialise in gourmet pastries, custom cakes, and fresh bread.

Our mission is to make delectable baked goods with love and attention to detail.
